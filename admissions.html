Admissions

Admission Process

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow in a nurturing environment. Our admission process is designed to be transparent, fair, and accessible to all sections of society.

Admissions are open throughout the year based on seat availability. We encourage parents to submit applications early to secure admission for their child.

Admission Criteria

  • Age-appropriate class admission (Pre-Primary to Class 7)
  • Previous academic records (for class 2 onwards)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Birth certificate (mandatory)
  • Address proof of residence
  • Recent passport-size photographs

Fee Structure

Transparent Pricing

We believe in providing quality education at affordable fees. Our fee structure is transparent with no hidden charges. Fee includes tuition, library, sports, and extracurricular activities.

For detailed fee structure, please contact the school office.

Scholarship Programs

We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students. Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, financial need, and other criteria.

  • Merit scholarships for toppers
  • Financial assistance for needy students
  • Sports scholarships

Payment Options

We offer flexible payment options to make education accessible:

  • Annual payment (with discount)
  • Quarterly payment plan
  • Monthly installment option
  • Online payment facility
  • Cash/Cheque/DD accepted

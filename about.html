About Our School

Welcome to Social Welfare English School

Established in 1967 with a vision to provide quality education to all, Social Welfare English School has been a beacon of learning and excellence for over 58 years. Located in Ward No.37, Kalyan Dombivli, Thane, Maharashtra, we believe in nurturing young minds and shaping them into responsible, compassionate, and successful individuals.

Our school is built on the foundation of strong values, academic excellence, and holistic development. We combine traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to create a dynamic learning environment where every student can thrive.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a student-centric approach, we ensure that each child receives personalized attention and opportunities to excel in academics, sports, arts, and other extracurricular activities.

Our Legacy

Since our inception in 1967, we have been committed to making quality education accessible to all sections of society. As a privately managed, unaided English medium school, our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and unwavering dedication to educational excellence. Under the leadership of our Head Trustee, DNYANESHWAR TATHU MARATHE, we continue to uphold the highest standards of education.

Over the years, we have produced countless successful alumni who have excelled in various fields and contributed meaningfully to society. Their achievements stand as a testament to the quality of education and values instilled at our institution.

Today, we continue to evolve and adapt to the changing educational landscape while staying true to our core mission of empowering young minds and building futures.

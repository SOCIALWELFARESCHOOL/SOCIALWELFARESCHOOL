Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Students need to meet age criteria, submit required documents including birth certificate, transfer certificate (if applicable), and previous academic records.

Do you offer transportation?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable school bus services covering major areas. Transport fees are separate from tuition fees.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure individual attention with 8 dedicated teachers providing personalized learning.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students based on academic performance and financial need.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow the State Board curriculum from Pre-Primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG) through Class 7.

What facilities does the school have?

Our school has 6 classrooms, library with 450+ books, playground, 3 computers, and proper sanitation facilities.
